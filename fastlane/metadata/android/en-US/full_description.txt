<b>Hibiki</b> is a Japanese immersion reading app for Android, built for learners who read extensively in Japanese.<br><br><li> 📖 <b>EPUB reader</b> powered by ッツ Ebook Reader with tap-to-look-up for instant dictionary queries</li><li> 📘 <b>Yomitan dictionaries</b> with pitch accent and frequency data — import your own dictionary files</li><li> 🃏 <b>Anki flashcard export</b> via AnkiDroid, with sentence context for frictionless sentence mining</li><li> 🎧 <b>Audiobook sync (Sasayaki)</b> — align audio with EPUB text using SRT/LRC/VTT/ASS subtitles for read-along playback</li><li> 🔍 <b>MeCab-powered tokenization</b> for accurate word boundary detection and deinflection</li><li> 📚 Read offline with a built-in bookshelf and reading progress tracking</li>
